How does it work?

A pool of pseudorandom numbers was generated when you loaded this page. When you hold your spacebar on roll your mousewheel over these numbers, some of them are taken out of the pool, these numbers are used to generate passwords and passphrases.

When you mash or faceroll your keyboard , your keystrokes generate even more random information that this app can use. We can then use this randomness, or entropy to create stronger, more random passwords.
